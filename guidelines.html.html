Staff Guidelines

Please read carefully before your first shift. These guidelines protect you, our clients, and the reputation we've built together.

01

Dress Code

✅
Black collared shirt or poloShort or long sleeve. Fully closed on chest. Not cropped.
✅
Black pantsLeggings are not considered pants — no exceptions.
✅
Comfortable closed-toe shoesYou'll be standing for long hours. Plan accordingly.

🌡️ Keep a black jacket in your car for cold nights. Bring a thermal water bottle to refill during your shift.

02

Arrival

🕐
Always arrive 15 minutes earlyBe ready before guests arrive.
📍
Event address is in the event descriptionCheck the pinned message or link sent with your confirmation.
📱
Running late?Contact us immediately via WhatsApp. Always communicate.
03

During the Event

📵
Phone on silentCheck messages only during breaks. Never while serving guests.
🎯
Be professional at all timesAttentive, warm, and focused. Your attitude reflects on all of us.
💬
Client questions about bookings or pricingSay: "Please speak with the coordinator about that."
04

Payments

⚠️ Important

If a client tries to pay you directly — do not accept it. Say: "Please speak with the coordinator about payments." All payments are processed by Aligna after the event.

💳
Payment every FridayVia Zelle or Venmo for all shifts completed that week.
05

Submitting Your Hours

After every shift, send your exact hours in the WhatsApp chat immediately after finishing.

Format
11:00am — 3:00pm
⚠️ No hours = No payment

Submit within 24 hours of your shift. No exceptions.

06

Cancellations

📅
Minimum 72 hours notice requiredNotify us via WhatsApp at least 3 days before your shift if you can't attend.
🚫
No-shows without noticeResult in immediate removal from roster. Emergencies happen — just communicate.
07

Tax & Work Authorization

📋
SSN or ITIN requiredAll staff must have a valid Social Security Number or Individual Taxpayer ID.
✍️
W-9 required before first shiftYou'll receive a digital form during onboarding. Legal requirement — no exceptions.
🇺🇸
Must be authorized to work in the USIf your status changes, notify us immediately.
08

Questions & Support

Reply in the WhatsApp chat anytime. We respond within a few hours. Never stay confused before a shift — always ask.
