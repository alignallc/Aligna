Introduction

A brand as good
as the pizza itself.

Una Pizza has something most businesses spend years trying to build — a real product, a loyal following, and an authentic story. What's missing is a brand system that communicates all of that clearly, consistently, and in a way that attracts the right clients.

"The goal isn't to look bigger than you are. It's to look exactly as good as you already are."

This proposal outlines a focused, practical plan to define your brand, build your digital presence, and give your social media manager the tools to represent Una Pizza consistently — without relying on guesswork.

02
Scope of Work

What we will
build together.

Five deliverables. Each one builds on the last. Together they create a complete brand and digital system that represents Una Pizza at its best.

01
Foundation
Brand Strategy Document
The foundation everything is built on. A clear, written definition of who Una Pizza is, who it serves, and where it's going.
  • Brand positioning — what makes Una Pizza distinct in the market
  • Ideal client profile — who you want to attract and why
  • Unique value proposition — the one thing only Una Pizza offers
  • 3-year vision — how the brand needs to be built to support future growth
  • Core brand values — 3 values that guide every decision
02
Identity
Logo Direction & Visual Identity
A complete visual brief that defines the logo direction, colour palette, and typography. Includes creative direction — I can design the initial logo concept or collaborate directly with your designer for final execution.
  • Colour palette — defined and approved before design begins
  • Typography system — primary and secondary fonts confirmed
  • Visual moodboard — 3 directions presented, 1 chosen
  • Logo concept — initial version created by me or handed to external designer with full brief
  • Logo review and refinement — we approve the final design together
  • All final files delivered: SVG, PNG, PDF — light and dark versions
03
Voice
Brand Voice Document
A practical guide that defines exactly how Una Pizza communicates — so every caption, reply, and message sounds like the same brand.
  • Tone of voice definition with real examples
  • Do's and don'ts for written communication
  • Caption templates for the 3 main content types
  • How to respond to inquiries, reviews, and comments
  • Language guide — when to use Spanish, Catalan, or English
04
Strategy
Social Media Strategy + Content Calendar
A complete system your social media manager can follow without guessing. Not management — a clear roadmap that makes management easy and results measurable.
  • 5 content pillars — what to post and why for each category
  • Monthly content calendar template in Google Sheets
  • Manager guidance document — rules, formats, frequencies
  • Reel formula that works for food and events content
  • 90-day goal — defined metrics to track progress
05
Digital
Website + Quote Page
A professional website that works as a business tool — not just a digital brochure. Clients can explore services and request a quote directly.
  • Home page — brand story, services overview, gallery, testimonials
  • 3 service pages — Pizza Events, Bar Service, Corporate Catering
  • Quote request forms — one per service with smart questions
  • Automatic quote email sent to client on submission
  • Mobile-first design — optimised for how clients actually browse
  • Available in Spanish and English
+ Graphic Design Support — Optional Add-on
Need additional branded materials? I can design social media templates, event menus, flyers, packaging mockups, or any other visual assets using the approved brand identity. Priced separately per project.
